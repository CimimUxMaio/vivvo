**Vivvo App** is a multi-role property management platform designed to streamline communication, payments, and financial transparency between **Tenants**, **Property Owners**, and **Managers**.

The app centralizes rent, expenses, contracts, and analytics into a single system, reducing friction and improving visibility for all parties involved.

---

## Core User Roles

Vivvo supports three distinct user roles, each with tailored features and permissions.

---

### 1. Tenant

Tenants use Vivvo to clearly understand and manage their financial obligations.

**Key capabilities:**

* View upcoming payments, including:
  * Rent (as defined in their contract)
  * Shared or individual expenses set by an Manager
* Access contract details and payment breakdowns
* Submit payment notifications by uploading payment receipts
* Track payment history and validation status

**Value for tenants:**

* Clear visibility of what is owed and why
* Proof-based payment tracking
* Reduced disputes and confusion

---

### 2. Owner

Owners use Vivvo to manage their properties, tenants, and income.

**Key capabilities:**

* Create and manage properties
* Create and manage rental contracts
* Assign tenants to properties and contracts
* View, list, and validate tenant-submitted payments
* Access dashboards and analytics, including:
  * Property performance
  * Income and gains
  * Payment status and trends

**Value for owners:**

* Centralized property oversight
* Easier payment validation
* Actionable financial insights

---

### 3. Manager

Managers oversee operational and shared expenses across properties.

**Key capabilities:**

* View and manage all assigned properties
* Submit expense requests with justification documents
* Notify relevant tenants of new expenses and payment requirements
* View, list, and validate submitted payments
* Monitor expense payment status across properties via dashboards

**Value for administrators:**

* Transparent expense management
* Simplified tenant communication
* Better control over operational finances

---

## Core Features Across the Platform

* Role-based access and permissions
* Contract-driven rent and payment rules
* Receipt-based payment notifications
* Validation workflows for payments
* Centralized dashboards and analytics
* Document uploads for contracts and expense justifications
* Notifications for payments, expenses, and validations

---

## Why Vivvo Matters

Vivvo reduces friction by:

* Aligning expectations between tenants, owners, and administrators
* Making payments and expenses transparent and traceable
* Providing real-time financial visibility through analytics
* Replacing fragmented tools (emails, spreadsheets, messaging apps)
